The dynamical model

The model generates a trajectory in a three-dimensional state space with co-ordinates . Quasi-periodicity of the ECG is reflected by the movement of the trajectory around an attracting limit cycle of unit radius in the -plane. Each revolution on this circle corresponds to one RR-interval or heart beat. Inter-beat variation in the ECG is reproduced using the motion of the trajectory in the -direction. Distinct points on the ECG, such as the P,Q,R,S and T are described by events corresponding to negative and positive attractors/repellors in the -direction.
